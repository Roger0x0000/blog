# 小试牛刀1
> U.S. President Donald Trump and Russian President Vladimir Putin held a meeting during the G-20 Summit in Germany on July 7, <font color=red>**while**</font> in a statement, Putin warned of a possible largescale conflict on the Korean Peninsula. <br>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**这是一个双黄蛋，**</font>
> 蛋黄1：U.S. President Donald Trump and Russian President Vladimir Putin held a meeting during the G-20 Summit in Germany on July 7 <br>

+ 主语1: <font color=red>**U.S. President Donald Trump**</font> —— <font color=red>**美国总统唐纳德·特朗普**</font>
+ 主语2: <font color=red>**Russian President Vladimir Putin**</font> —— <font color=red>**俄罗斯总统弗拉基米尔·普金**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**held**</font> —— <font color=green>**举行了**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**a meeting**</font> —— <font color=blue>**一场会晤**</font>
+ 状语: <font color=orange>**during the G-20 Summit in Germany on July 7**</font> —— <font color=orange>**七月七日在德国于G20峰会期间**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**七月七日在德国于G20峰会期间，美国总统唐纳德·特朗普与俄罗斯总统弗拉基米尔·普金举行了一场会晤。**</font>

> 蛋黄2：while in a statement, Putin warned of a possible largescale conflict on the Korean Peninsula <br>

+ 状语1: <font color=orange>**while in a statement**</font> —— <font color=orange>**与此同时在一则声明当中**</font>
+ 主语: <font color=red>**Putin**</font> —— <font color=red>**普京**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**warned of**</font> —— <font color=green>**警告了**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**a possible largescale conflict**</font> —— <font color=blue>**一场可能的大规模冲突**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=cyan>**on the Korean Peninsula**</font> —— <font color=cyan>**朝鲜半岛上**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**与此同时在一则声明当中，普京警告了朝鲜半岛上一场可能的大规模冲突**</font>

+ 整体翻译：<font color=purple>**七月七日在德国于G20峰会期间，美国总统唐纳德·特朗普与俄罗斯总统弗拉基米尔·普金举行了一场会晤，与此同时在一则声明当中，普京警告了朝鲜半岛上一场可能的大规模冲突。**</font>

> The museum houses a diverse collection of more than 2,300 examples of European art from 13th century religious paintings to more modern ones. <br>


+ 主语: <font color=red>**The museum**</font> —— <font color=red>**该博物馆**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**houses**</font> —— <font color=green>**藏有**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**a diverse collection**</font> —— <font color=blue>**一批丰富的收藏品**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=cyan>**of more than 2,300 examples / of European art / <font color=orange>from</font> 13th century religious paintings / <font color=orange>to</font> more modern ones**</font> —— <font color=cyan>**从13世纪宗教画作到更现代的那些欧洲艺术的超过2300幅代表作的**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**该博物馆藏有从13世纪宗教画作到更现代的那些欧洲艺术的超过2300幅代表作的一批丰富的收藏品。**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**定语中的from...to...短语，不能倒序。**</font>



> US bombers have visited North Korea's east coast and have demonstrated their military options to defeat any threat, the Pentagon has said. <br>


+ 主语: <font color=red>**US bombers**</font> —— <font color=red>**美国轰炸机**</font>
+ 谓语1: <font color=green>**have visited**</font> —— <font color=green>**已造访**</font>
+ 宾语1: <font color=blue>**North Korea's east coast**</font> —— <font color=blue>**朝鲜东海岸**</font>

+ 连词: <font color=orange>**and**</font> —— <font color=orange>**并**</font>


+ 谓语2: <font color=green>**have demonstrated**</font> —— <font color=green>**已展示**</font>
+ 宾语2: <font color=blue>**their military options**</font> —— <font color=blue>**其军事选择**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=cyan>**to defeat any threat**</font> —— <font color=cyan>**足以战胜任何威胁的**</font>

+ 状语1: <font color=orange>**the Pentagon has said**</font> —— <font color=orange>**五角大楼称**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**五角大楼称，美国轰炸机已造访朝鲜东海岸并已展示足以战胜任何威胁的其军事选择。**</font>


> Liberties give us the freedom to act, or to refuse, or to think in certain ways, but liberties do not always mean the freedom to do these things without any interference. <br>

+ 解析: <font color=purple>**这是一个双黄蛋，**</font>
> 蛋黄1： Liberties give us the freedom to act, or to refuse, or to think in certain ways <br>



+ 主语: <font color=red>**Liberties**</font> —— <font color=red>**自由权**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**give**</font> —— <font color=green>**赋予**</font>
+ 宾语1: <font color=blue>**us**</font> —— <font color=blue>**我们**</font>
+ 宾语2: <font color=blue>**the freedom**</font> —— <font color=blue>**自由**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=orange>**to act, / or to refuse, / or to think in certain ways**</font> —— <font color=orange>**做事，或拒绝，或以特定方式去思考的**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**自由权赋予我们做事，或拒绝，或以特定方式去思考的自由**</font>


> 蛋黄2：but liberties do not always mean the freedom to do these things without any interference <br>

+ 连词: <font color=cyan>**but**</font> —— <font color=cyan>**但**</font>
+ 主语: <font color=red>**liberties**</font> —— <font color=red>**自由权**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**do not always mean**</font> —— <font color=green>**并不总是意味着**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**the freedom**</font> —— <font color=blue>**自由**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=orange>**to do these things without any interference**</font> —— <font color=orange>**不受任何干涉地去做这些事情的**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**但自由权并不总是意味着不受任何干涉地去做这些事情的自由。**</font>

+ 整体翻译：<font color=purple>**自由权赋予我们行动，或拒绝，或以特定方式去思考的自由，但自由权并不总是意味着不受任何干涉地去做这些事情的自由。**</font>


# 集训换骨蜕变练习

## 练习1


> The plants, animals, crops and microorganisms（微生物）that are the bedrock（基石）of food production are in decline, according to a UN study. <br>

+ 定语: <font color=cyan>**that are the bedrock of food production**</font> —— <font color=cyan>**作为食物生产基石的**</font>
+ 主语: <font color=red>**The plants, animals, crops and micro-organisms**</font> —— <font color=red>**植物、动物、农作物和微生物**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**are**</font> —— <font color=green>**是**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**in decline**</font> —— <font color=blue>**处于减少中**</font>
+ 插入语: <font color=orange>**according to a UN study**</font> —— <font color=orange>**根据联合国的一项研究**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**根据联合国的一项研究，作为食物生产基石的植物、动物、农作物和微生物正在减少。**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**主语-The plants, animals, crops and micro-organisms（植物、动物、农作物和微生物）；定语-that are the bedrock of food production（作为食物生产基石的），修饰主语，翻译时放在主语前；谓语-are（是）；宾语-in decline（处于减少中）；插入语-according to a UN study（根据联合国的一项研究），指示消息来源，翻译时放在句子开头。**</font>

> A woman has said she was left alone on an Air Canada（加拿大航空）plane after falling asleep during a flight.  <br>

+ 主语: <font color=red>**A woman**</font> —— <font color=red>**一位女性**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**has said**</font> —— <font color=green>**称**</font>
+ 宾语从句主语: <font color=red>**she**</font> —— <font color=red>**她**</font>
+ 宾语从句谓语: <font color=green>**was left**</font> —— <font color=green>**被留下了**</font>
+ 宾语从句状语: <font color=orange>**alone on an Air Canada plane after falling asleep during a flight**</font> —— <font color=orange>**在一次飞行期间在睡着后在一架加拿大航空的飞机上独自**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**一位女性称她在一次飞行期间在睡着后在一架加拿大航空的飞机上独自被留了下来。**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**主语-A woman（一位女性）；谓语-has said（称），后接宾语从句。宾从主she（她）；宾从谓-was left（被留下了）；宾从状-alone on an Air Canada plane after falling asleep during a flight（在一次飞行期间在睡着后在一架加拿大航空的飞机上独自），内部根据介词切块倒序，整体倒序至宾从谓前。**</font>

> Kobe Bryant（科比·布莱恩特）'s eldest daughter paid tribute（致敬） to her father and her late sister.  <br>


+ 主语: <font color=red>**Kobe Bryant's eldest daughter**</font> —— <font color=red>**科比·布莱恩特的大女儿**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**paid...to...**</font> —— <font color=green>**把...给了...**</font>
+ 宾语1: <font color=blue>**tribute**</font> —— <font color=blue>**悼念**</font>
+ 宾语2: <font color=orange>**her father and her late sister**</font> —— <font color=orange>**她的父亲和她已故的妹妹**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**科比·布莱恩特的大女儿把悼念给了她的父亲和她已故的妹妹。（科比·布莱恩特的大女儿向她的父亲和她已故的妹妹致以悼念。）**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**句子的主语-Kobe Bryant's eldest daughter（科比·布莱恩特的大女儿），谓语-paid to整体看作谓语（把…给了），宾语1-tribute（悼念），宾语2-her father and her late sister（她的父亲和她已故的妹妹）。整句话没有需要倒序的地方。**</font>


> They built the two specialized hospitals in less than 15 days. <br>

+ 主语: <font color=red>**They**</font> —— <font color=red>**他们**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**built**</font> —— <font color=green>**修起了**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**the two specialized hospitals**</font> —— <font color=blue>**两所专业化医院**</font>
+ 状语: <font color=orange>**in less than 15 days**</font> —— <font color=orange>**在不到15天里**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**他们在不到15天里修起了两所专业化医院。**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**句子的主语-They（他们），谓语-built（修起了），宾语-the two specialized hospitals（两所专业化医院），后面是一个状语-in less than 15 days（在不到15天里），可以作状语1，放在句首，也可以看作状语2译后倒序前置到谓语前。**</font>


> 许多老年人仍然帮着照看孙辈。 <br>


+ 主语: <font color=red>**许多老年人**</font> —— <font color=red>**Many elder people**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**仍然帮着照看**</font> —— <font color=green>**still help to look after**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**孙辈**</font> —— <font color=blue>**grandchildren**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**Many elder people still help to look after grandchildren.**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**这个句子里的谓语比较长。主语-许多老年人（Many elder people），谓语-仍然帮着照看（still help to look after），宾语-孙辈（grandchildren），典型的主谓宾结构。**</font>

> 在北京举行的世界通信技术5G大会上，来自全球的专家和学者们共同探讨5G的未来。  <br>

+ 状语1: <font color=orange>**在北京举行的世界通信技术5G大会上**</font> —— <font color=orange>**On the world telecommunication 5G conference held in Beijing**</font>
+ 主语1: <font color=cyan>**专家（们）**</font> —— <font color=cyan>**experts**</font>
+ 主语2: <font color=red>**学者们**</font> —— <font color=red>**scholars**</font>
+ 定语1: <font color=blue>**来自全球的**</font> —— <font color=blue>**from all over the world**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**（正在）探讨**</font> —— <font color=green>**are discussing**</font>
+ 状语2: <font color=orange>**共同**</font> —— <font color=orange>**together**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**未来**</font> —— <font color=blue>**the future**</font>
+ 定语2: <font color=blue>**5G的**</font> —— <font color=blue>**of 5G**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**On the world telecommunication 5G conference held in Beijing, experts and scholars from all over the world are discussing together the future of 5G.**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**解析：今天的句子我们来看看最近风头正劲的5G。逗号的前面，是一个长长的状语1-在北京举行的世界通信技术5G大会上（On the world telecommunication 5G conference held in Beijing），句子的主干在逗号后面；主语有2个，主语1-专家（们）（experts），连接词-和（and），主语2-学者们（scholars），谓语-（正在）探讨（are discussing）, 状语2-共同（together），宾语-未来（the future）,定语2-5G的（of 5G）。整个句子成分比较多，需要注意2次定语的后置和1次状语2的后置**</font>





## 练习2



>  How we handle change is the key to happiness. <br>

+ 主语: <font color=red>**How we handle change**</font> —— <font color=red>**我们如何处理变化**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**is**</font> —— <font color=green>**是**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**the key**</font> —— <font color=blue>**关键**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=orange>**to happiness**</font> —— <font color=orange>**幸福的**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**我们如何处理变化是幸福的关键。**</font>


> At least 10 people have died after drinking alcohol-based （含酒精的） sanitizer（消毒剂）. <br>


+ 主语: <font color=red>**At least 10 people**</font> —— <font color=red>**至少10人**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**have died**</font> —— <font color=green>**死亡**</font>
+ 状语2: <font color=orange>**after drinking alcohol-based sanitizer**</font> —— <font color=orange>**在饮用含酒精的消毒剂后**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**至少10人在饮用含酒精的消毒剂后死亡。**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**主语-At least 10 people (至少10人)，谓语-have died (死亡)，状语2-after drinking alcohol-based sanitizer (在饮用含酒精的消毒剂后)，翻译时倒序到谓语前。**</font>

> At least 10 people have died after drinking alcohol-based （含酒精的）sanitizer（消毒剂） after liquor shops in a village in an Indian state were closed. <br>


+ 主语: <font color=red>**At least 10 people**</font> —— <font color=red>**至少10人**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**have died**</font> —— <font color=green>**死亡**</font>
+ 状语2: <font color=orange>**after drinking alcohol-based sanitizer after liquor shops in a village in an Indian state were closed**</font> —— <font color=orange>**在印度某邦的一个村庄的酒类商店被关闭后在饮用含酒精的消毒剂后**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**至少10人在印度某邦的一个村庄的酒类商店被关闭后在饮用含酒精的消毒剂后死亡。**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**主语-At least 10 people (至少10人)，谓语-have died (死亡)，状语2- after drinking alcohol-based sanitizer after liquor shops in a village in an Indian state were closed (在印度某邦的一个村庄的酒类商店被关闭后在饮用含酒精的消毒剂后)，翻译时倒序到谓语前，这个长状语内部，由介词after切分，分成after drinking alcohol-based sanitizer 和after liquor shops in a village in an Indian state were closed两块，翻译时内部倒序。其中after liquor shops in a village in an Indian state were closed是一个after引导的状语从句，状从主-liquor shops（酒类商店）, 状从谓- were closed (被关闭)，状从定-in a village in an Indian state（在印度某邦的一个村庄），内部由in切分，内部倒序，翻译时整体倒序到状从主前，翻译为“在印度某邦的一个村庄的酒类商店被关闭后”。其中after drinking alcohol-based sanitizer内部顺序翻译，翻译为“在饮用含酒精的消毒剂后”，最后翻译时长状语整体倒序到谓语前。**</font>


> Climate change is one of the greatest challenges of our time. <br>

+ 主语: <font color=red>**Climate change**</font> —— <font color=red>**气候变化**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**is**</font> —— <font color=green>**是**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**one**</font> —— <font color=blue>**一个**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=orange>**of the greatest challenges of our time**</font> —— <font color=orange>**我们这个时代最大的挑战的**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**气候变化是我们这个时代的最大的挑战之一。**</font>
+ 解析: <font color=purple>**主语- Climate change (气候变化)，谓语- is (是)，宾语- one (一个)，定语- of the greatest challenges of our time (我们这个时代最大的挑战的)。也可以把one of the greatest challenges (最大的挑战之一) 看作是一个整体的宾语。**</font>




>  Climate change is one of the greatest challenges of our time and its adverse （负面的）impacts undermine（破坏） the ability of all countries to achieve sustainable development. <br>


+ 主语: <font color=red>**Climate change**</font> —— <font color=red>**气候变化**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**is**</font> —— <font color=green>**是**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**one**</font> —— <font color=blue>**一个**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=orange>**of the greatest challenges of our time**</font> —— <font color=orange>**我们这个时代最大的挑战的**</font>
+ 连接词: <font color=cyan>**and**</font> —— <font color=cyan>**并且**</font>
+ 主语: <font color=red>**its adverse impacts**</font> —— <font color=red>**它的负面影响**</font>
+ 谓语: <font color=green>**undermine**</font> —— <font color=green>**破坏了**</font>
+ 宾语: <font color=blue>**the ability**</font> —— <font color=blue>**能力**</font>
+ 定语: <font color=orange>**of all countries to achieve sustainable development**</font> —— <font color=orange>**实现可持续发展的所有国家的**</font>
+ 按照公式翻译: <font color=blue>**气候变化是当今时代的最大挑战之一，它的负面影响破坏了实现可持续发展的所有国家的能力。**</font>
